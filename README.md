# Car Rental Pricing Analysis — Ireland (April 2026)

## Overview
Competitor rate analysis across Dublin, Cork, Shannon and Galway airports 
comparing Europcar against Hertz, Avis, Enterprise, Budget and Sixt.

## What's Inside
- Europcar rates across 4 locations, 4 vehicle classes, 2 seasons
- Competitor rate-shopping data collected via rentalcars.com
- Variance Analysis using XLOOKUP, AVERAGEIF, MINIFS formulas
- Pivot Summary by location and vehicle class
- Dashboard with charts and 9 analyst insights

## Key Findings
- Enterprise consistently prices above market across all locations
- People Carrier segment shows inverted seasonality
- Galway has limited competition (Enterprise and Budget only)
- Go Car sister concern provides seven-seater alternative at lower cost
- People Carrier inverted seasonality driven by corporate travel demand and    limited fleet availability
- Business growth opportunity identified for Europcar — People Carrier
  expansion to Cork and Shannon
  
## Tools Used
Microsoft Excel — XLOOKUP, AVERAGEIF, MINIFS, IF, cross-sheet references
